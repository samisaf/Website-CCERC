Dr. Patel is an intensivist and pulmonologist at the University of Virginia, where he serves as an assistant professor in the Division of Pulmonary and Critical Care Medicine. He is a clinician educator with interests in mechanical ventilation, procedural training, communication skills, feedback, leadership development, and the ways in which the learning environment shapes trainee well-being and resilience. He directs the critical care mechanical ventilation curriculum at UVA and teaches extensively at the bedside as well as in formal educational settings across disciplines. He also teaches at several local courses and national conferences, including ATS, CHEST, and the Harvard Macy Program. Dr. Patel mentors medical education-focused residents and fellows, supporting their growth as clinicians, educators, and emerging scholars.
